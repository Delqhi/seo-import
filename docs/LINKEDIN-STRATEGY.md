# LinkedIn Strategy 2026

## Why LinkedIn Matters for B2B & Professional Reach

**LinkedIn has 1+ billion users** and is the #1 platform for B2B lead generation. LinkedIn posts get 20x more engagement than other platforms for professional content.

### LinkedIn Algorithm 2026

**Key Ranking Signals:**
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Dwell Time** | #1 | Keep users reading (long posts, documents) |
| **Comments** | #2 | Spark professional discussion |
| **Reposts** | #3 | Create shareable insights |
| **Profile Visits** | #4 | Optimize profile for conversions |
| **Connection Requests** | #5 | Network actively |

### LinkedIn Content Strategy

#### Post Types That Go Viral
1. **Text-only posts** — Personal stories, lessons learned (highest reach)
2. **Document/Carousel posts** — PDF slides with tips (highest engagement)
3. **Video posts** — Professional insights, tutorials
4. **Poll posts** — Industry opinions, market research
5. **Case studies** — Real results with data
6. **Behind the scenes** — Company culture, building process

#### Optimal LinkedIn Post Structure
```
Line 1: Hook (Bold statement or question)
Line 2: Context (Why this matters)
Line 3-8: Value (Step-by-step, insights, data)
Line 9: Summary (Key takeaway)
Line 10: CTA (Comment, Follow, Visit Website)
```

#### Hook Examples
1. "I turned down a $500K offer to build OpenSIN. Here's why:"
2. "After 3 years building AI agents, here's what I learned:"
3. "The biggest mistake I see founders make:"
4. "We just hit 100 AI agents. Here's our playbook:"

### LinkedIn Optimization Checklist
- [ ] **Headline**: Value proposition + keywords (not just job title)
- [ ] **About**: Story + results + CTA with link
- [ ] **Featured**: Link to website, best posts, media
- [ ] **Banner**: Professional branding with value proposition
- [ ] **Posting**: 3-5 posts/week, daily engagement
- [ ] **Comments**: Comment on 10-20 posts daily
- [ ] **Connections**: 20-50 new connections/week
- [ ] **Newsletter**: LinkedIn Newsletter for thought leadership

### LinkedIn → Website Funnel
```
Viral Post → Profile Visit → Featured Section → Link Click → Website
```

1. **Post**: "How we built 100 AI agents" (text-only, 2000 characters)
2. **Comments**: Reply to every comment with value
3. **Featured**: "Read the full technical deep dive → me.delqhi.com"
4. **About**: "Building OpenSIN → opensin.ai"

### Quick Wins
1. **Post Tuesday-Thursday 8-10 AM** — Highest engagement
2. **Use text-only posts** — 2-3x more reach than links
3. **Put link in comments, not post** — Algorithm penalizes external links
4. **Tag 3-5 relevant people** — Increases visibility
5. **Use 3-5 hashtags** — #AI #OpenSource #Tech
6. **Engage for 30 min after posting** — Boosts distribution
7. **Write newsletters** — Subscribers get notified
8. **Share company updates** — Employee advocacy
9. **Post carousels as PDFs** — Highest engagement format
10. **Comment on large accounts** — Piggyback on their reach
