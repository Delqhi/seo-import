# Content Automation Workflow 2026 — The "Content Machine"

> **How to produce 45+ viral posts per week without burning out.**

## The Problem
The **3x3 Framework** (3 accounts × 3 posts/day) requires **45 posts per week**.
Doing this manually is impossible for one person long-term. You need a **Content Machine**.

## The Solution: AI + Automation Stack

### Phase 1: Ideation (AI-Assisted)
**Goal:** Generate 50+ content ideas per week.

**Tools:**
- **ChatGPT/Claude:** "Give me 50 viral video ideas about [Topic] using these hook formulas: [List formulas]"
- **TikTok Creative Center:** Check trending sounds and hashtags.
- **Google Trends:** Find rising topics.
- **AnswerThePublic:** Find what people are asking.

**Workflow:**
1. Run AI prompt to generate 50 ideas.
2. Filter top 10 based on relevance and trend data.
3. Expand each idea into a 30-second script.

### Phase 2: Creation (Batch Production)
**Goal:** Film 45 videos in 4 hours.

**Setup:**
- **Lighting:** Ring light or window light.
- **Audio:** Wireless lapel mic (crucial for retention).
- **Teleprompter:** Use an app like "Teleprompter for Video".
- **Background:** Clean, consistent, or screen recording setup.

**Batching Strategy:**
1. **Script Day:** Finalize 45 scripts (AI does 80%, you do 20%).
2. **Filming Day:** Record all 45 videos in one session.
   - Change shirt every 15 videos (looks like different days).
   - Use "Green Screen" effect for screen recordings.
3. **Editing Day:** Edit in CapCut using templates.
   - Create 3 "Master Templates" for your 3 accounts.
   - Auto-captions are mandatory.

### Phase 3: Distribution (Automated)
**Goal:** Post to 4 platforms automatically.

**Tools:**
- **Repurpose.io:** Auto-posts TikTok → YouTube Shorts, IG Reels, Snapchat.
- **Buffer/Later:** Schedule text posts (X, LinkedIn).
- **Zapier:** Connect everything.

**Workflow:**
1. Post to TikTok (Account 1, 2, 3).
2. Repurpose.io detects new video.
3. Auto-removes watermark.
4. Auto-posts to YouTube, IG, Snapchat.
5. **Result:** 1 upload → 4 platforms.

### Phase 4: Analytics & Iteration
**Goal:** Optimize based on data.

**Tools:**
- **TikTok Analytics:** Watch time, retention graph.
- **YouTube Studio:** CTR, average view duration.
- **Google Analytics:** Traffic from social to website.

**Weekly Review:**
1. Identify top 3 performing videos.
2. Analyze *why* they worked (Hook? Topic? Format?).
3. Create 5 variations of the winners.
4. Delete or archive underperforming formats.

## AI Prompts for Content Generation

### Idea Generation
```text
Act as a viral content strategist. Generate 20 video ideas about [Topic] using these hook formulas:
1. "Stop doing [X]. Do this instead..."
2. "I tried [X] for 30 days. Here's what happened..."
3. "Nobody talks about [surprising fact]..."
4. "This [tool] changed everything for me..."
5. "3 [things] about [topic] that will blow your mind..."

For each idea, provide:
- Hook (First 3 seconds)
- Body (Value proposition)
- CTA (Visual demonstration)
```

### Script Writing
```text
Write a 30-second script for a TikTok video about [Topic].
Structure:
- 0:00-0:03: Hook (Must stop the scroll)
- 0:03-0:20: Value (Step-by-step or insight)
- 0:20-0:25: Proof (Data or example)
- 0:25-0:30: Visual CTA (Show URL being typed)

Tone: Authentic, raw, helpful. No corporate jargon.
```

### Hook Optimization
```text
Here is my video hook: "[Your Hook]"
Make it 3x more viral by:
1. Adding a curiosity gap
2. Using stronger emotional triggers
3. Making it more specific
4. Adding a visual element suggestion

Give me 5 variations.
```

## Scaling Beyond One Person

### When to Hire
- You're consistently hitting 100K+ views/week.
- You're spending >4 hours/day on content.
- You have a proven conversion funnel.

### Who to Hire
1. **Video Editor:** $500-$1000/mo (Part-time)
   - Cuts raw footage, adds captions, formats for platforms.
2. **Content Manager:** $1000-$2000/mo (Full-time)
   - Schedules posts, replies to comments, tracks analytics.
3. **Scriptwriter:** $500-$1000/mo (Part-time)
   - Researches trends, writes scripts using AI + human touch.

### Management System
- **Notion/Trello:** Content calendar and script approval.
- **Google Drive:** Raw footage and assets.
- **Slack/Discord:** Team communication.
- **Loom:** Feedback on videos.

## The "Set and Forget" System

Once optimized, your Content Machine runs on autopilot:
1. **AI** generates ideas/scripts weekly.
2. **You** film in 1 batch session.
3. **Editor** processes videos.
4. **Repurpose.io** distributes automatically.
5. **Manager** handles engagement and analytics.

**Result:** You focus on product/business. Content machine drives traffic 24/7.
