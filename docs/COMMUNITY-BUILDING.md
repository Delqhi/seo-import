# Community Building Strategy 2026

> **Turn followers into a tribe that promotes you for free.**

## Why Community Matters

**A community of 1,000 true fans is worth more than 1M followers.**

Communities drive:
- **Word-of-mouth marketing** (free promotion)
- **User-generated content** (free content creation)
- **Product feedback** (better products)
- **Customer loyalty** (higher LTV)
- **AI citations** (Reddit, forums are heavily cited by AI)

## The Community Flywheel

```
Content → Attracts Members → Members Create Content → Attracts More Members → Product Sales → Fund Better Content
```

### Step 1: Seed the Community (Days 1-30)
- **Invite your top 100 followers** from social media
- **Create 10 discussion threads** to start conversations
- **Post daily** to keep the community active
- **Respond to every message** personally
- **Host weekly events** (AMA, workshop, Q&A)

### Step 2: Empower Members (Days 31-60)
- **Identify active members** and give them roles
- **Create member spotlights** (feature their work)
- **Start challenges** (30-day coding challenge, etc.)
- **Launch a referral program** (invite friends, get rewards)
- **Delegate moderation** to trusted members

### Step 3: Scale (Days 61-90)
- **Automate onboarding** (welcome messages, rules)
- **Create content channels** (resources, wins, help)
- **Host monthly events** (guest speakers, workshops)
- **Launch paid tier** (exclusive content, direct access)
- **Integrate with product** (community feedback → features)

## Engagement Tactics

### Daily
- **Welcome new members** by name
- **Ask open-ended questions** ("What are you working on?")
- **Share wins** (yours and members')
- **Post discussion prompts** ("Hot take: [controversial opinion]")

### Weekly
- **AMA (Ask Me Anything)** sessions
- **Member spotlight** (feature one member)
- **Challenge/check-in** ("What did you accomplish this week?")
- **Resource share** (tools, articles, tutorials)

### Monthly
- **Guest speaker** or workshop
- **Community vote** (next topic, feature request)
- **Milestone celebration** (member count, achievements)
- **Feedback session** (what's working, what's not)

## Community Guidelines

### Essential Rules
1. **Be respectful** — No harassment or hate speech
2. **No spam** — No self-promotion without permission
3. **Stay on topic** — Keep discussions relevant
4. **Help others** — Answer questions when you can
5. **Have fun** — This is a community, not a corporation

### Enforcement
- **First offense:** Warning
- **Second offense:** Temporary mute
- **Third offense:** Ban
- **Zero tolerance:** Immediate ban for harassment/hate speech

## Measuring Community Health

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target | Tool |
|--------|--------|------|
| **Daily Active Members** | >20% of total | Discord Analytics |
| **Messages per Day** | >50 | Discord Analytics |
| **New Members per Week** | >10 | Platform Analytics |
| **Retention Rate** | >60% at 30 days | Platform Analytics |
| **Event Attendance** | >15% of members | Event Platform |
| **Paid Conversion** | >20% of active members | Stripe |

### Red Flags
- **Declining activity** — Need fresh content/events
- **High churn** — Community not providing value
- **Spam increase** — Need better moderation
- **Negative sentiment** — Address issues immediately
