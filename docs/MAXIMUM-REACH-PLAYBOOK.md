## The Viral Content Formula

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)
- **Pattern interrupt** — Break the expected
- **Curiosity gap** — Create an open loop
- **Specific promise** — "In 60 seconds you'll know X"
- **Emotional trigger** — Surprise, shock, humor

### Body (Next 15-45 Seconds)
- **Deliver on the hook** — Immediately
- **Add value** — Teach, entertain, or inspire
- **Keep it moving** — Cut every 2-3 seconds
- **Visual variety** — Change angles, add text, B-roll

### CTA (Last 3-5 Seconds)
- **Single action** — One clear next step
- **Low friction** — Easy to do
- **Value exchange** — "Follow for more X"
- **Urgency** — "Before this gets deleted..."

## Key Metrics to Track

### Reach Metrics
- **Total views across all platforms** — Overall reach
- **Unique viewers** — Actual audience size
- **Follower growth rate** — Audience building speed

### Engagement Metrics
- **Average engagement rate** — (likes + comments + shares) / views
- **Comment sentiment** — Positive vs negative
- **Share rate** — How often content is shared
- **Save rate** — How often content is saved

### Conversion Metrics
- **Website clicks** — Traffic from social
- **Email signups** — Lead generation
- **Product signups** — Conversions
- **Revenue** — Business impact
