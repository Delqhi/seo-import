# X (Twitter) Strategy 2026

## Why X Matters for AI & Tech Reach

**X is the #1 platform for AI/Tech discourse.** 87% of AI researchers and developers use X as their primary news source. AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity heavily index X for real-time information.

### X Algorithm 2026 — How It Works

**Key Ranking Signals:**
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Dwell Time** | #1 | Keep users on your tweet (threads, long posts) |
| **Replies** | #2 | Spark conversation, ask questions |
| **Reposts** | #3 | Create shareable, valuable content |
| **Likes** | #4 | Emotional resonance |
| **Bookmarks** | #5 | High-value, reference-worthy content |
| **Profile Visits** | #6 | Bio optimization, pinned tweet |

### X Content Strategy

#### Tweet Types That Go Viral
1. **Threads** — Deep dives, tutorials, case studies (10-15 tweets)
2. **Hot Takes** — Contrarian opinions on industry trends
3. **Build in Public** — Daily updates on your project
4. **Data/Stats** — Original research, surprising numbers
5. **Curated Lists** — "Top 10 tools for X", "Best resources for Y"
6. **Visual Tweets** — Charts, graphs, screenshots with context
7. **Polls** — Engagement bait, market research

#### Thread Structure for Maximum Reach
```
Tweet 1: Hook (Curiosity + Promise + Visual)
Tweet 2-8: Value (Step-by-step, data, examples)
Tweet 9: Summary (TL;DR)
Tweet 10: CTA (Follow, Bookmark, Visit Website)
```

#### Hook Formulas That Work
1. "I spent 100 hours studying X. Here's what I learned:"
2. "Most people are wrong about X. Here's the truth:"
3. "How I went from [A] to [B] in [Time]:"
4. "X is dead. Here's what's replacing it:"
5. "10 tools that will save you 20 hours/week:"

### X Optimization Checklist
- [ ] **Bio**: Clear value proposition + keywords + link
- [ ] **Pinned Tweet**: Your best thread/case study
- [ ] **Header Image**: Branding + social proof
- [ ] **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 tweets/day, 1 thread/week
- [ ] **Engagement**: Reply to 10-20 large accounts daily
- [ ] **Lists**: Create lists of industry leaders to engage with
- [ ] **Spaces**: Host or join weekly audio discussions
- [ ] **Newsletter**: Link to newsletter in bio/tweets

Viral Thread → Profile Visit → Pinned Tweet → Link Click → Website

### Measuring X Success
- **Impressions** — Reach measurement
- **Engagement Rate** — (Likes + Replies + Reposts) / Impressions
- **Profile Visits** — Interest in your brand
- **Link Clicks** — Traffic to website
- **Follower Growth** — Audience building

### Quick Wins
1. **Post threads on Tuesday-Thursday 8-10 AM** — Highest engagement
2. **Reply to @elonmusk, @sama, @ylecun** — Piggyback on reach
3. **Use "Bookmark this for later"** — Boosts algorithmic ranking
4. **Tag relevant accounts** — Increases visibility
5. **Post native video** — X promotes video content
6. **Use polls** — High engagement, low effort
7. **Quote tweet with value** — Add perspective to viral tweets
8. **Engage for 15 min after posting** — Boosts initial distribution
