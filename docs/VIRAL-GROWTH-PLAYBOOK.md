## 🚨 THE 3x3 DISTRIBUTION FRAMEWORK

**The #1 mistake: Posting occasionally on one account.**

In 2026, the algorithm is a lottery where you can buy more tickets through volume.

### The 3x3 Strategy (PROVEN)
```
3 Accounts × 3 Posts/Day × 5 Days/Week = 45 posts/week
```

**Why this works:**
- Every post is a data point telling you what the audience wants
- If one account's reach dips, the other two keep traffic flowing
- Law of large numbers guarantees at least one hook catches fire
- You dominate the "For You" page across multiple accounts

**Real Case Study: DuckMath**
- Founder: Maddox Schmidt
- Strategy: 3 TikTok accounts, 3 posts/day each
- Result: 150,000 daily active users → $120,000 exit
- Key insight: "I was posting three times a day, 5 days a week... and that was the crazy marketing stunt that worked."

### Account Strategy

**Account 1: Main Brand**
- Professional, polished content
- Behind-the-scenes, tutorials
- Links to main website

**Account 2: Personal/Founder**
- Raw, authentic, phone-on-screen
- "Day in the life" content
- Hot takes, opinions

**Account 3: Niche/Topic**
- Focused on one specific topic
- Educational content
- Tips, tricks, hacks

## 🎣 HOOK ITERATION — Engineering the "Dopamine Hit"

**Volume without iteration is just noise.**

The most successful videos (5M+ views) are often "crappy" in production but **elite in visual hooks**.

### Hook Types That Work (2026 Data)

| Hook Type | Visual Element | Psychological Trigger | Expected Result |
|-----------|---------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| **The "Secret" Hook** | Low-quality phone-on-screen | Exclusivity/Gatekeeping | **High Share Rate** |
| **The "Dopamine" Hook** | Fast-paced game/action | Instant Gratification | **High Retention** |
| **The "Problem/Solution"** | Side-by-side comparison | Relatability | **High Conversion** |
| **The "POV" Hook** | Relatable scenario | Identification | **High Engagement** |
| **The "Before/After"** | Transformation visual | Curiosity | **High Completion** |

### Proven Hook Formulas (2026)

1. **"Stop doing [X]. Do this instead..."** + phone recording screen
2. **"I tried [X] for 30 days. Here's what happened..."** + before/after visual
3. **"Nobody talks about [surprising fact]..."** + laptop screen recording
4. **"This [tool/method] changed everything..."** + showing the tool in action
5. **"3 [things] about [topic] that will blow your mind..."** + fast cuts
6. **"POV: You're a [role] and [situation]..."** + relatable scenario
7. **"The truth about [topic] that [industry] doesn't want you to know..."** + whisper tone
8. **"I spent [X hours/dollars] on [thing]. Was it worth it?"** + showing the thing

### The Raw Authenticity Rule

**Phone-on-screen > Polished corporate video**

- Film your laptop screen with your phone
- Create a sense of raw, authentic discovery
- Edit quickly in CapCut
- Don't over-produce — "crappy" videos often go viral
- The hook must match the audio trend

## 🎯 THE VISUAL CTA — Converting Scrollers to Users

**Millions of views are a vanity metric if they don't convert.**

### Old CTA (Ineffective)
- "Link in bio" (verbal mention)
- "Check out our website"
- Subtle branding

### New CTA (3x More Effective)
- **Show URL being typed** on screen
- Screen recording of visiting the website
- Visual demonstration of the product
- "Go to [URL] right now" + show it

**Key Insight:** Showing the URL being typed is **3x more effective** than a verbal mention. Show, don't just tell.

### CTA Framework
```
0:00-0:03 — HOOK (Stop the scroll)
0:03-0:20 — VALUE (Deliver on the hook)
0:20-0:25 — VISUAL CTA (Show URL being typed)
0:25-0:30 — URGENCY ("Before this gets deleted...")
```
