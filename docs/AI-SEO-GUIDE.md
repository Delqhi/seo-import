AI search traffic grew **1,200% in 2025**. By 2026, **87.4% of AI referral traffic** flows through ChatGPT. 85.7% of brands are completely invisible to AI search engines. This guide covers everything you need to dominate both traditional AND AI search.

## The 7 Pillars of AI SEO (GEO)

### 1. Content Structure
- **Q&A headings** — Format headings as questions AI would answer
- **Front-load data** — First 30% of content captures 44.2% of citations
- **Named entities** — 15+ per page (people, products, companies, technologies)
- **Direct answers** — Write like answering a specific question, not filling word count
- **Structured lists** — AI extracts list items more reliably than paragraphs

### 2. Brand Authority
| Signal | Correlation | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| YouTube presence | 0.737 | Create video content, embed in posts |
| Web mentions | 0.664 | Guest posts, PR, partnerships |
| Reddit mentions | 0.423 | Genuine community participation |
| Backlinks | 0.218 | Quality over quantity |

### 3. Technical Readiness
- ✅ Allow AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended)
- ✅ Schema markup (Organization, Article, FAQ, Product minimum)
- ✅ `llms.txt` file for LLM context
- ✅ `ai.txt` for AI-specific instructions
- ✅ Fast loading (<2s), mobile-friendly
- ✅ Sitemap.xml with all pages
- ✅ RSS feed for content syndication

### 4. Platform-Specific Optimization
- **ChatGPT**: Bing ranking, FAQ schema, fresh content
- **Perplexity**: Reddit presence, recency, community signals
- **Claude**: Long-form authoritative content, academic attribution
- **Google AI**: E-E-A-T signals, structured data, Shopping Graph

### 5. Freshness
- Update important pages quarterly minimum
- Add "Last updated" dates
- Refresh data points and statistics
- New content = 3.2x more citations

### 6. Cross-Platform Presence
- Website (primary)
- YouTube (highest correlation)
- Reddit (Perplexity's #1 source)
- LinkedIn (professional authority)
- GitHub (developer credibility)
- News/Publications (media authority)

### 7. Measurement
- **Visibility rate**: % of relevant prompts where brand appears
- **Dark AI traffic**: Up to 80% appears as "Direct" in GA4
- **Referral traffic**: From known AI domains
- **NOT**: AI ranking positions (nearly random per query)

## The 6 Most Common AI SEO Mistakes

1. **Blocking AI crawlers** in robots.txt
2. **Ignoring brand mentions** on third-party sites
3. **Chasing AI ranking positions** instead of visibility rate
4. **Publishing stale content** (18+ months old)
5. **Missing structured data** (82% of cited domains have it)
6. **No measurement system** for AI visibility
